Before you continue

What you need to submit a claim

You’ll need:

  • to know who bought your equipment or adaptation (this could be yourself, your employer, or the supplier)
  • the date of when your equipment or adaptation was bought, (you can find this on the invoice, receipt or order confirmation of your equipment or adaptation)
  • a digital receipt or invoice, or if you have a paper receipt or invoice you’ll need a smart phone, camera, or scanner to upload them
  • the bank details of who bought your equipment or adaptation

If you can’t digitally upload your receipt or invoice you’ll need to submit your claim with the paper form by post. Your paper form will have been sent to you.
