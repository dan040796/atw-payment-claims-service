What you’ll need to send your claim

You’ll need to send a claim for each type of support you get. For example, if you use a British Sign Language interpreter and have a driver, you’ll need to send 2 claims.

You’ll need to tell us:

  • the dates you had support
  • how many hours of support you had on each date
  • the name and email address of a workplace contact who we can ask to confirm the support you’re claiming for
  • the details of the person or company who needs paying, including bank details if we’ve not paid them before

This can include anyone who paid for your support worker, such as yourself, a family or friend, your employer or the company that provided your support worker.

You’ll need to send us an invoice or receipt for any support worker costs you’re claiming for.

If you are self-employed or work at the same organisation as the person providing the support

You’ll need to send us a digital copy of a signed timesheet or payslip for the person who provided your support.
