Information about your grant

We’ll help pay for support to make it easier for you to do your job. This support can be used whilst you work as a Finance Analyst at Deaf Action.

What support you’ll get

Type of grant
Travel to Work support
Travel type
Taxis to & from work for 10 journeys per week
Cost of support
£45.00 per journey
Agreed employer contribution
£7.50 per journey
Total amount we can pay towards this claim
£37.50 per journey

You should have already received a copy of your grant approval letter by post. You can also view a copy of your grant approval letter in your account.

Your support starts from 05 March 2021 until 05 March 2024 if your needs do not change.
