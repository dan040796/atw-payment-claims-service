Send your claim by post

It’s quickest to send your Access to Work claims online. If you cannot send your claims online, you can send Access to Works claims by post.

You’ll need to:

  • fill in the paper claim form that was sent with your Access to Work award letter
  • include any receipts or invoices we ask for in the claims form

You can download a claim form and print it off if you need one.

You should post your claim to Access to Work:

Access to Work Service Centre
Harrow Jobcentre
Mail Handling Site A
Wolverhampton
WV98 1JE

You’ll receive a text message or email to let you know when we’ve got your claim form.
