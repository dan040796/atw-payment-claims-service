Start a claim

You’ll need to provide receipts or invoices if you’re claiming for support worker costs. You must provide digital copies of your receipts or invoices to submit a claim online. You’ll need to scan or take a photo of any paper receipts or invoices ready to upload them if you have any.

If you cannot provide digital copies of your receipts and invoices, you’ll need to submit your claims by post.

After you submit a claim

Your claim will be sent to someone at your workplace to review. We’ll contact the person whose details you give us in your claim form. Your workplace contact needs to be someone like your line manager or HR manager as they will need to approve your claim.

We’ll let you know whether your claim is approved by your workplace contact using the contact method you chose. If your claim is approved, we’ll process your claim and pay the money into the bank account you chose.

If your workplace contact disagrees with your claim, we’ll contact you to let you know what to do next.

You’ll be able to see updates about your Access to Work grant and the progress of your claims in your account homepage.
