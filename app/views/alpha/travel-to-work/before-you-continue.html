Before you continue

What you’ll need to submit a claim

You’ll need:

  • to tell us the date you travelled
  • the bank details of who needs to be paid back
  • the name and email address of who we need to contact to confirm your work travel

If you travelled by taxi

You’ll also need:

  • to tell us how many journeys you did each day
  • an invoice or receipt for date you travelled, or a receipt or invoice that contains all the dates. If you have a paper copy, you’ll have to take a photo or scan it
  • to tell us the total cost of your work travel for the month you are claiming for

If you didn’t travel by taxi

You’ll need:

  • to tell us the date and mileage for each day you travelled

If you can’t digitally upload your receipt or invoice you’ll need to submit your claim with the paper form by post. Your paper form will have been sent to you.
