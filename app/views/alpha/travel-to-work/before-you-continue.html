Before you continue

What you’ll need to submit a claim

You’ll need to know:

  • the date(s) that you travelled for work
  • the details of the person who needs to be paid back – you’ll also need to give us their bank details if we’ve not paid this person before
  • the name and email address of a workplace contact who we can contact to confirm each journey you’re claiming for

If you’re claiming for travel by taxi

You’ll also need to:

  • tell us how many journeys you made each day
  • tell us the total cost of your work travel for the month you are claiming for
  • send us an invoice or receipt for any journeys you’re claiming for

You can either send us 1 receipt or invoice that contains all of the journeys you’re claiming for, or separate receipts and invoices for each single journey. You’ll need a digital copy of your receipts or invoices to submit a claim online. You should scan or take a photo of any paper receipts or invoices ready to upload them if you have any.

If you did not travel by taxi

You’ll also need to tell us the total mileage travelled on each date.
