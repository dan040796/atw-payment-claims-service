Volunteer to take part in research

You can send an email to atwonlinefeedback@dwp.gov.uk if you’re happy for someone to contact you to discuss your experience using this service.

Tell us your name and if you’d prefer to be contacted by email or telephone.

If you’re happy for us to contact you by telephone, also include:

  • your telephone number
  • dates and times it would be convenient for one of the team to call
We cannot reply to queries about Access to Work or individual claims.

If you have a question about the service or need help with a claim, contact the Access to Work helpline.

How we will use your details

We will only use your email address or telephone number to contact you. We will not share your details or use them for anything else.
