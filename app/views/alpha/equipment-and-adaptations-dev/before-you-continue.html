What you’ll need to submit a claim

You can claim for multiple pieces of equipment or adaptations.

You'll need to:

  • tell us the date you purchased your equipment or adaptation – you can find this on the receipt or invoice
  • send us an invoice or receipt for the equipment and adaptation you’re claiming for
  • tell us details of the person or company who needs to be paid back, including bank details if we’ve not paid them before

This can include anyone who paid for your equipment or adaptation, such as yourself, a family or friend, your employer or the company who provided your equipment or adaptation.
