Start a claim

You’ll need to provide receipts or invoices if you’re claiming money back for any equipment or adaptations. You must provide digital copies of your receipts or invoices to submit a claim online. You’ll need to scan or take a photo of any paper receipts or invoices ready to upload them if you have any.

If you cannot provide digital copies of your receipts and invoices, you’ll need to submit your claims by post.

After you submit a claim

Your claim has been sent. If your claim is approved, we’ll process your claim and pay the money into the bank account you chose.

If there’s any problems with your claim, we’ll contact you to let you know what to do next.

You’ll be able to see updates about your Access to Work grant and the progress of your claims in your account.
