What you will need to review and confirm a claim

Your employee has completed an Access to Work claim, before payment is made, you must review and confirm their claim.

{% if (data["journey-type"]=="supportworker") %}

You will need:

  • to know their claim number, this will have been sent to you in an email
  • to know the dates your employee received support and for how long
  • to review their claim and check the invoices or receipts they uploaded
{% elseif (data["journey-type"]=="traveltowork") %}

You will need:

  • to know their claim number, this will have been sent to you in an email
  • to know the dates your employee travelled to work
  • to review their claim and check the invoices or receipts if they travelled by taxi
