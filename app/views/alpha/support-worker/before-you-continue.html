Before you continue

What you’ll need to submit a claim

You’ll need to know:

  • the date(s) that you travelled for work
  • the personal and bank details of the person who needs to be paid back
  • the name and email address of a workplace contact who we can contact to confirm each journey you’re claiming for

If you are self-employed or work at the same organisation as the person providing the support

You will also need:

  • a payslip or a signed timesheet for the person who provided you support, if you have a paper copy you’ll need to take a photo or scan them in

If you can’t digitally upload your receipt or invoice you’ll need to submit your claim with the paper form by post. Your paper form will have been sent to you.
