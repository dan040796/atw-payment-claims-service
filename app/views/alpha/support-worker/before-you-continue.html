Before you continue

What you’ll need to submit a claim

You’ll need to submit a claim for each type of support you get. For example, if you use a British Sign Language interpreter and have a driver, you’ll need to submit 2 claims.

For each claim, you’ll need to know:

  • the date(s) you had support
  • how many hours of support you had on each date
  • the details of the person who needs to be paid back – you’ll also need to give us their bank details if we’ve not paid this person before
  • the name and email address of a workplace contact who we can contact to confirm the support costs you’re claiming for

You’ll also need to send us an invoice or receipt for any support worker costs you’re claiming for. You’ll need a digital copy of your receipts or invoices to submit a claim online. You should scan or take a photo of any paper receipts or invoices ready to upload them if you have any.

If you are self-employed or work at the same organisation as the person providing the support

You’ll need to send us a payslip or signed timesheet for the person who provided you support. This will need to be a digital copy.
