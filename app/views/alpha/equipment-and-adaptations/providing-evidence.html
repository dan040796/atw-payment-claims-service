How to get a digital copy of your paper receipt or invoice

You can include multiple receipts or invoices when scanning or taking photos of receipts or invoices. This will save you time when it comes to uploading them later on.

You must make sure you scan or take photos of both the front and back of receipts.

Taking photos of receipts or invoices

Make sure:

  • you're in a well-lit place
  • you include the whole receipt or invoice in the image
  • you take the photo from above and not at an angle
  • the photo is not blurry

Scanning receipts or invoices

Make sure:

  • you include the whole receipt or invoice in the scan
  • the scan is not blurry
