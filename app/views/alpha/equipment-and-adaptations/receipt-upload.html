Upload your receipt or invoice

To upload your receipt or invoice you’ll need to save your digital copy, your photo or your scan on the device you are using.

You’ll need to:

  1. Click on the upload button
  2. Select the files you want to upload

Make sure that it is the correct file format (PNG,JPEG,PDF,GIF,DOCX), the document is not password protected and the total files are smaller than 20mb.
