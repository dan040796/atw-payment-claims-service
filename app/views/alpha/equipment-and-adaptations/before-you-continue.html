Before you continue

What you need to submit a claim online

You must make your claim within 9 months of the purchase date of your equipment or adaptation. You can claim for multiple pieces of equipment or adaptations.

You will need:

  • to tell us the date your equipment or adaptation was purchased, you can find this on the receipt or invoice
  • to tell us the bank details of who needs to be paid back
  • a digital receipt or invoice for all the equipment and adaptations

If you have paper copy or your receipt or invoice you cannot upload, you will need to submit your claim with the paper form by post. Your paper form will have been sent to you.
