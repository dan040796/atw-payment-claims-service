What you’ll need to make a claim

You can claim for more than one piece of specialist equipment.

You'll need to:

  • tell us the date you purchased the equipment – you can find this on the receipt or invoice
  • send us an invoice or receipt for the equipment you’re claiming for
  • tell us details of the person or company we need to pay, including bank details if we’ve not paid them before
The person or company we need to pay could be anyone who paid for your equipment. This includes yourself, a friend, a family member, your employer or the company who provided the equipment.
