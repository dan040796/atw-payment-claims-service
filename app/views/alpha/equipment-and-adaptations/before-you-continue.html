What you need to submit a claim online

You must claim for equipment and adaptations within 9 months of the purchase date. You can claim for multiple pieces of equipment or adaptations.

You'll need to:

  • tell us the date you purchased your equipment or adaptation – you can find this on the receipt or invoice
  • tell us the bank details of the company or person who needs to be paid back
  • send us an invoice or receipt for the equipment and adaptation you’re claiming for

You’ll need a digital copy of your receipts or invoices to submit a claim online. You should scan or take a photo of any paper receipts or invoices ready to upload them if you have any.
