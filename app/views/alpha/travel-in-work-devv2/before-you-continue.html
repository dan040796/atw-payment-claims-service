What you’ll need to send your claim

You’ll need:

  • the dates of each journey you took during work
  • the postcode of the place where each journey started and finished
  • the total cost of each journey you're claiming for
  • details of the person or company who needs to be paid back, including bank details if we’ve not paid them before
The person or company who needs paying back could be yourself, a family member or friend, your employer or a transport company.

You also need to send us one of the following:

  • a receipt or invoice that contains all the journeys you’re claiming for
  • separate receipts and invoices for each journey
If you're an employee

You’ll also need the name and email address of a workplace contact who we can ask to confirm each journey you’re claiming for. This needs to be someone like your line manager or HR manager.
