Before you continue

What you’ll need to submit a claim

You’ll need:

  • to tell us the date you received support and for how long
  • an invoice or receipt for each day you received support, if you have a paper copy, you’ll have to take a photo or scan it in
  • the bank details of who needs to be paid back
  • the name and email address of who we need to contact to confirm you received support
  • to submit a claim for every type of support you receive, for example if you have BSL support and a driver you’ll need to submit 2 claims

If you are self-employed or work at the same organisation as the person providing the support

You’ll need:

  • a payslip or a signed timesheet for the person who provided you support, if you have a paper copy you’ll need to take a photo or scan them in

If you can’t digitally upload your receipt or invoice you’ll need to submit your claim with the paper form by post. Your paper form will have been sent to you.
